Openexo is a multiplanetary project that encompasses real factual data of land, past, present and future, of history made and in the making all into a very unique, value based NFT design.

Each NFT represents a virtual land-owning opportunity, with specific coordinates, data and characteristics. Openexo’s NFT’s are limited, unique and available with factual historical data all stored on the Cardano blockchain.

Past

Land parcels with legendary historical meanings are available to those interested in owning some of the very best NFT’s offered by Openexo. When you own these NFT’s, you unlock future available lamd opportunities not available to any other.

Present

The goal of the human race is to be a multiplanetary species, until this is a reality, land parcels available today represent where humans have visited, where rovers have been, where machines have traveled. These parcels will undoubtedly be discovered and cherished as items to hold on to.

Future

With all the excitement around the globe, who really knows where we will go, what we will see, the future is unknown. These land parcels are for those who have interest in areas of our solar system that humans may visit. Those who own these land parcels will have the “special” benefit of being notified to own “new” parcels with updated information.
